 # Touch Gesture Controls - Implemented
 
 ## Completed Features
 
 - ✅ Two-finger pinch to zoom (via OrbitControls)
 - ✅ Single-finger swipe to rotate camera  
 - ✅ Tap to select characters
 - ✅ Double-tap to deselect
 - ✅ Long-press to activate move mode
 - ✅ Tap floor spots to move avatar
 - ✅ Visual feedback (ripples, pulsing markers)
 - ✅ Mobile-specific hint text
 
 ## Files Created
 - `hooks/useTouchGestures.ts` - Touch gesture detection
 - `TouchFeedback.tsx` - Ripple and indicator components
 - `FloorSpotMarker.tsx` - Floor spots for movement
